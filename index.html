About Us

Our Story

NexLynk started from a simple observation that engineering students in Kenya often graduate without meaningful practical exposure.

I personally experienced this struggle after graduating with a BSc. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.Finding a meaningful attachment was difficult,and when I finally did,I realized how much more I could have learned earlier.

That's why I decided to create NexLynk,to help students access real industry experiences,connect with mentors,and bridge the gap between theory and hands-on skill.

Our Mission

To create pathways for engineering students and graduates to gain real,hands-on industrial experience that transforms their academic knowledge into practical expertise.

We believe that every engineering student deserves the opportunity to apply their learning in real-world settings,building confidence and competence before entering the workforce.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every engineer graduates skilled,confident,and ready to build innovative solutions for our nation's challenges.

We envision a future where the gap between academia and industry is bridged, creating a pipeline of highly competent engineering professionals who drive technological advancement across Africa.
